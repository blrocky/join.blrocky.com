Want to join Barely Legal?



Have you ever thought: "I love Rocky Horror so much, I wish I could join the cast!"??

Then we've got good news for you:



You totally can!


Barely Legal strives to put on a screen-accurate show - we want to match what's happening on the screen as closely as possible with props, costumes, make-up and gigantic floating lips.

To do that, we need dedicated, hard-working people to join us. It's a lot of work, but it's more fun than you can imagine (unless you have some sort of Super Heavy Duty Imagining Device).

Barely Legal has been performing non-stop all over the San Francisco Bay Area since 1995. We do shows in San Jose, Albany, Berkeley, Petaluma and other cities throughout northern California. We are currently looking for:
